\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Every move usuable by a player, eidos, or monster is handled here. Must be associated with a \doxylink{class_combatant}{Combatant} Sub-\/\+Class. 

Combatants have associated Moves that have a function to validate casting of the spell, and execution of the actual spell. every instance of a \doxylink{class_move}{Move} is aware of the combatant for the sake of being able to figure out who the target of x \doxylink{class_combatant}{Combatant} is, and for applying status effects.
